Broken Access Control: IDOR 

 Title    : IDOR leads to add/remove jobs to victim's job selections 
 Weakness : Broken Access Control - IDOR
 Severity : Medium 
 Impact   : An Attacker can add/remove jobs to victim's job selections  
   
 Steps To Reproduce:
 1. Register two accounts (attacker & victim) 
 2. Login to attacker account  
 3. Click add job button to add job to job selection 
 4. Now change the "ConId" parameter to victim number 
 5. In the response you will find "UUID" for victim account 
 6. Now add the "ConId" and "UUID" for victim account and you successfully add/remove jobs to victim's job selections

 1. https://portswigger.net/web-security/access-control/idor
